About Us

Solitude Farms, a division of Solitude Standard Limited (RC: 6932206), is dedicated to excellence in agriculture.

Registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020, we are situated on a 250-acre farmland in Ota,Ogun State, Nigeria.

We specialize in the cultivation and distribution of high-quality agricultural produce.

Our commitment to sustainable farming and innovation ensures we deliver top-notch products to our clients andinvestors.

Since our inception in 2020, we have been steadfast in our mission to transform the agricultural landscape.
